## Cap stage 

- 開始分化
- Dental organ
  - OEE (Outer enamel epi.)
  - IEE
  - Stellate reticulum
- dental papilla

## Early bell stage 

- Dental lamina 斷開
- Ameloblast
  - 向下生成牙釉質
- Cervical loop 反折
- Odontoblast (中胚層)
  - 向上生成pre dentine

先Dentine 後 Enamel
:::
