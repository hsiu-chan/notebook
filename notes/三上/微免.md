1. Alcohols 
   - non-enveloped viruses(沒外套膜)不能殺
   - Spore, some fungi 不能殺
2. Chlorhexidine (CHX)
   - ==德恩奈漱口水能殺 SARS-CoV-2==
3. Triclosan 
   - 高露潔用這個
   - 但是好像會致癌

## Disinfection and Sterilization

1. Oxidizing
   - 3-6% Disinfection
   - 10-25% Sterilization
2. Chlorine compounds
   - 蔡衍明乾杯
3. Aldehydes
   - 蛋白質軟化
4. Radiation
   - 痛扁DNA (TT Dimer)
5. 抗生素
   - Protosil (Sulfanilamide)
   - Penicillin

  - Lethal toxin = PA63+LF
